# Our Pantry to Woolies

## What this does

This little Chrome extension takes the shopping list you copied from Our Pantry
(with the "Copy for Woolies" button) and adds those products to your Woolworths
online trolley in one go. You always get to review and untick items first —
nothing goes in your trolley until you press the button.

## Getting the files onto your PC

1. Open the project on GitHub in your browser.
2. Click the green **Code** button, then **Download ZIP**.
3. Unzip the downloaded file (right-click → **Extract All** on Windows).
4. Inside the unzipped folder, find the folder called **`extension`** — that's
   the one you'll need in the next step. Remember where it is.

## Installing it in Chrome (one-time)

1. Open Chrome and type `chrome://extensions` into the address bar.
2. Turn on **Developer mode** — it's a toggle in the top-right corner.
3. Click **Load unpacked** (top-left).
4. Pick the **`extension`** folder from the previous section.
5. Done — you'll see "Our Pantry to Woolies" in your list of extensions. You
   can pin it to the toolbar with the puzzle-piece icon if you like.

**A note on the warning banner:** Chrome may show a message like "Disable
developer mode extensions" when you restart it. That is normal and expected
for any extension loaded this way — you can dismiss it.

## Using it

1. In Chrome, go to **woolworths.com.au** and **sign in** to your account.
2. In Our Pantry (on your phone or anywhere), open the List tab and tap
   **Copy for Woolies**.
3. Back in Chrome, click the extension's icon while you're on the Woolworths
   site.
4. Paste your list into the box.
5. Review the items — untick anything you don't want this time.
6. Click **Add … items to trolley**, and check the message it shows you.

Then open your trolley on Woolworths as usual to check everything, choose your
delivery or pickup, and pay — all of that stays completely in your hands.

## Good to know

- It only ever **adds** to your trolley. It never removes anything, never
  checks out, and never touches payment, delivery, or your account settings.
- If some items couldn't be added (out of stock, discontinued), it tells you
  exactly how many landed and how many didn't.
- **Desktop Chrome only.** Chrome on Android phones and tablets does not
  support extensions at all, so this needs a computer.
